Unlocking Opportunity in Self-Storage

Uncompromising StandardsUnparalleled Experience

Merit Hill is a woman-owned, real estate investment firm focused on acquiring, owning, and managing self-storage facilities across the U.S.

Merit Hill Capital is one of the most experienced institutional self-storage owners in the United States. Led by a founder who has been exclusively managing assets in the sector since 2006, the firm focuses on a resilient asset class, distinctive and carefully sourced deal flow, and detailed value-add management strategies. Merit Hill Capital provides investors and sellers with an experienced team driven by integrity, a strong work ethic, and deep industry knowledge.

A History of Exceeding Expectations1

With our refined investment process, deep network of relationships, and a strong reputation, Merit Hill has been one of the most active self-storage investors, raising 7 vehicles, since its founding in 2016.

A Repeatable and Extremely Disciplined Approach to Self-Storage Investing

Merit Hill is one of the largest buyers of individual self-storage properties in the U.S. and is extremely selective as to which properties it acquires. Our team consistently seeks to generate strong, risk-adjusted returns through a repeatable process and approach that includes and benefits from:

Asset Optimization

We reposition assets through active implementation of value-add initiatives, including conversions, expansions, and energy conservation projections, analyzed and executed by our experienced asset management team.

Strong Management

We actively collaborate with best-in-class third-party managers to maximize performance and drive improved operations at the property level.

Diversification

To decrease risk, we invest in assets across multiple geographies within the U.S. primarily through small transactions.

Evaluation

Using robust data sources, and our refined investment analysis and review process, our experienced investment team identifies the best opportunities.

Relationships

Our extensive industry relationships drive differentiated deal flow to identify the strongest investment opportunities.
